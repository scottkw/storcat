### Integration Finding

**--help output stream inconsistency (non-critical, affects CLIP-04)**
- `version.go` (Phase 8) and `open.go` (Phase 10): --help writes to `os.Stderr`
- `list.go`, `search.go`, `create.go` (Phase 9), `show.go` (Phase 10): --help writes to `os.Stdout`
- UNIX convention: --help exits 0, writes to stdout
- Impact: Cosmetic; does not affect functionality or test results

## Critical Integration Issue

### APPLE_ID Missing from Credential Rotation Runbook
- **Severity:** High
- **File:** docs/runbooks/credential-rotation.md
- **Issue:** release.yml line 77 references `${{ secrets.APPLE_ID }}` for `xcrun notarytool --apple-id`. The runbook documents 5 Apple secrets but omits APPLE_ID. Without this secret, notarization fails on first tag push.
- **Affected Requirements:** CRED-06 (runbook incomplete), SIGN-02 (notarization), SIGN-03 (stapling)
- **Fix:** Add APPLE_ID to runbook Section 1 with description and `gh secret set` command. Update total from 9 to 10.
